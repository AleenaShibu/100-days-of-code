KURIAKOSE ELIAS COLLEGE MANNANAM

Kuriakose Elias College, Mannanam, is a general degree college located in Mannanam, Kottayam district, Kerala. It was established in the year 1964. The college is affiliated with Mahatma Gandhi University.[1] This college offers different courses in arts, commerce and science.
